About the company

Story

Home electronics made-to-measure and tuned-in with personal taste is becoming more and more the core need of all those who, exhausted after strenuous daily work and constant rush, are happy to be back home. That is why the right choice of devices is of outmost importance in order to feel relaxed, comfortable and cosy at home.

Since 2015, we have been one of the main local suppliers of quality electronics made in Germany, Japan and China which has always enabled us to meet a wide variety of tastes and demands. Our supply ranges from the so-called traditional items to various new trends which create current standards in the culture of dwelling.

This exceptional variety of supply is supported by professional and expert counselling and 3D design and planning.
